Khachapuri Recipe

Description

Khachapuri (ხაჭაპური / Хачапури) is a Georgian dish of cheese-filled bread. It typically involves bread molded into an oval shape, fillied in the center with a mixture of cheeses and egg. The bread crust is typically torn off and deeped into the cheese.

Ingredient List

For 4 Servings

For the dough:

  • Flour (3 and a half cups)
  • Water (1 cup)
  • Milk (Half a cup)
  • Sugar (1 tablespoon)
  • Garlic Powder (1 tablespoon)
  • Yeast (1 teaspoon)
  • Salt (1 teaspoon)

For the filling:

  • Eggs (4 + 1 for egg wash)
  • Farmer's Cheese (1 and a half cups)
  • Shredded Mozzarella (1 and a half cups)
  • Feta Cheese (1 and a half cups)
  • Butter (optional)

Preperation

  1. In a stand mixer bowl, combine the dry dough ingredients: Salt, Yeast, Sugar, and Flour
  2. Heat Water and Milkto 115°F then pour it in the bowl of dry ingredients
  3. Knead the dough with the hook until it's smooth and elastic (around 1 minute), then add oil and knead for another minute
  4. Drizzle some oil into the bottom of a deep bowl and place the dough inside. Cover with plastic wrap and set aside in a warm place for about 1 hour and 15 minutes
  5. Remove the plastic wrap and knead the dough a few times. Cover it with plastic again and leave for another 30 minutes. Combine the Farmer's Cheese, Mozzarella, and Feta in the meantime
  6. Remove the dough from the bowl and place it on a floured surface, cutting it into 4 equal pieces. Speread each piece of dough into a circle about 9 inches in diameter. Then roll 2 opposite sides towards the center so it ends up with a boat-like shape. Then pinch the corners together
  7. Transfer the dough on to a baking sheet lined with greased parchment paper. Beat 1 egg with a teaspoon of water, then brush the dough with the egg wash. Then stuff each piece of dough with the cheese mixture.
  8. Bake in the oven at 450°F for about 15 minutes or until the crust becomes golden brown.
  9. Make a well in the center of each khachapuri with the back of a spoon and drop an egg into each and add butter (optional). Then return to the oven for another 5-10 minutes while the egg is still runny.
